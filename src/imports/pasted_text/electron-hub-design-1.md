Design a formal, structured, and professional web-based system UI for **"Electron Hub"**, the official **Online Enrollment and AI-Assisted Strand Recommendation System** of **Electron College of Technological Education (Malanday)**.

The system must include a **public-facing institutional website** (Home, About, Gallery, Contact, Enrollment) before authentication, and a **protected student system** after login.

---

DESIGN PRINCIPLES:

* Institutional, academic, and authoritative
* Clean, minimalist, and grid-based layout
* No promotional or startup-style design
* Strong emphasis on clarity, structure, and usability

---

COLOR SYSTEM (STRICT IMPLEMENTATION):

* PRIMARY COLORS:

  * Blue (#1E3A8A)
  * Red (#B91C1C)

USAGE:

* Blue → navigation bars, headers, primary structure

* Red → key actions, highlights, active states, important buttons

* NEUTRALS:

  * White (#FFFFFF)
  * Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
  * Dark Gray text

RULES:

* Maintain balance between blue and red across all pages
* Red must be visible but controlled (used for emphasis)
* Avoid gradients; use flat colors
* Ensure strong readability and contrast

---

TYPOGRAPHY:

* Font: Inter or Poppins
* Headings: Semi-bold
* Body: Regular
* Consistent hierarchy and spacing

---
